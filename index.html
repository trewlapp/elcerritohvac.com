Common signs include unusual noises like banging or rattling, insufficient heating or cooling throughout your home, unexpected increases in your energy bills, poor airflow or hot and cold spots in different rooms, frequent system cycling on and off, and strange odors coming from your vents. If you notice any of these issues, it's best to call a professional for a thorough inspection.
We recommend scheduling professional HVAC maintenance at least twice a year — once in the spring before the cooling season begins and once in the fall before heating season. Regular maintenance helps prevent unexpected breakdowns, improves energy efficiency (lowering your utility bills), extends the lifespan of your equipment, and keeps your home's air quality healthy.
Choosing the right size HVAC system is crucial for energy efficiency and home comfort. The appropriate size depends on your home's square footage, insulation quality, number and type of windows, ceiling height, and the local climate. A system that is too small will struggle to maintain comfort, while an oversized system will short-cycle and waste energy. Our NATE-certified technicians perform a detailed load calculation to recommend the perfect system for your home.
